Call for Sponsorships

IEEE CogMI welcomes and highly appreciates sponsorship support from interested Industry, Government or Academia. Various levels of sponsorships and benefits are as follows. If you have interest beyond or outside these packages please do contact us (Amirreza Masoumzadeh: amasoumzadeh [AT] albany [DOT] edu).

Based on the amount you donate, you will qualify for one of the following IEEE CogMI Corporate Sponsorship levels (in US dollars or equivalent):

Sponsorship
Level		 Sponsorship Amount Main Sponsor Benefits
Platinum >= $10,000
  • Special recognition as Platinum Supporter at the conference banquet
  • Poster (from Company/Institution) display indicating Platinum Sponsor at the registration booth/area
  • Two complimentary conference registrations for the conference
  • Any number of company/institution-provided items in conference bag
Gold >= $5,000
  • Recognized as Gold Supporter at the banquet
  • One complimentary conference registrations for the conference
  • Display space during the conference
  • Two company/institution-provided items in conference bag
Silver >=$2,500
  • Recognized as Silver Supporter at the banquet
  • One company/institution-provided item in conference bag
In- kind Sponsor support of a particular activity at the conference
  • We offer the opportunity to sponsor specific events and activities, e.g. student grants, coffee breaks, lunch, reception, or conference banquet. In these cases the sponsor name is prominently displayed during the event and an explicit "thank you" is mentioned at the welcome note and the banquet of the event.
