Important Registration Information

The deadline for registration can be seen in the important date web page and the fees table below. Registrations apply to all 3 co-located conferences IEEE CIC 2025, IEEE TPS 2025, IEEE CogMI 2025. Authors must choose correct conference when registering. Attendees can choose any conference and are welcome to attend co-located conferences/workshops.

IMPORTANT:

  • Paper Registration: At least one author per accepted paper/poster MUST register at the IEEE Member or Non IEEE Member rate on or before the deadline. Student IEEE Member rate is not available for Author Registration; i.e., student co-authors cannot register for a paper at the Student IEEE Member rate (please note that Paper Registration is important to cover the publication and other costs).

    Papers of authors who fail to attend and present their papers will immediately be removed from further consideration for inclusion in the proceedings and the planned journal special issues.

  • Each Paper Registration includes the submission of up to ten (10) pages for full paper (Regular, Application and Invited papers), and up to four (4) pages for Posters.

  • Multiple Papers: To submit up to four (4) additional papers, the cost is US$450 per paper (early bird rate) and $500 after the cutoff date.

  • Each registration includes access to all three conferences and co-located workshops, a USB proceeding, lunches, social banquet, and reception.

Transfer and Cancellation Policy: Cancellation request must be done by September 30, 2025. There will be an administrative fee of $100 for cancellation. Transfer of registration to another person must be received by September 30, 2025 (for free); after September 30, 2025 there will be a fee of $100 for transfers.

The REGISTRATION LINK is: Joint Conference Registration (Coming Soon)

Please email ieeecs-reg+cic@computer.org if you need any registration assistance. For questions regarding author submissions, please contact the conference organizers: jarrett.julian@gmail.com
